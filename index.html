Methodology:

  • Take a seed set of 1000 books from the Goodreads Best Books List.
  • Remove entries that are neither fiction nor single books. In the future, we will try removing books that are in series, childrens books, and books published within the past n years.
  • Applied a weighted Bayesian rating, with scaling (m) parameter = 2500 ratings, and average rating set equal to 4.0.
